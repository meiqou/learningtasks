My Top 10 Favorite Songs

A Toshi no Aki

Lamp

The song captures a bittersweet atmosphere of a fleeting autumn romance and the longing for what once was. The lyrics blend visual imagery, emotions, and nostalgic elements to convey the evocative feelings associated with the changing seasons and the transient nature of love in the city.

▶

EVERYTHING

The Black Skirts

The song "Everything" by The Black Skirts is about the deep love and appreciation the singer has for their significant other. They express how their partner is their everything, their summer, their dream and that they will take them as they are.

▶

Virgin Suicide

宇宙ネコ子

"Virgin Suicide" speaks to the complexities that come with romantic relationships and how change can impact love. Through its wistful lyrics, the song captures the sense of longing that comes with nostalgia, as the singer reflects on a time when love was childlike and straightforward.

▶

For Lovers

Lamp

The song "恋人へ (For Lovers)" by Lamp seems to be a nostalgic and reflective piece about love, loss, and memories. A a bittersweet reminder that time flies by quickly and that nothing lasts forever, but also a testament to the powerful bond that love can create between two people.

▶

Your face

Wisp

The song "Your face" by Wisp appears to explore themes of infatuation, longing, and the complexity of emotions from an unrequited love or one-sided relationship. Capturing the complexity of emotions and the struggles of desiring someone who may not feel the same way.

▶

Somewhere

the neverminds

Express themes of anticipation, uncertainty, nostalgia, and enduring love in a relationship. They touch upon waiting for the future, reminiscing about shared memories, accepting potential separations, and reaffirming love despite the passage of time.

▶

My Jinji

落日飛車 (Sunset Rollercoaster)

"My Jinji" is a heartfelt love song that expresses the singer's reliance on Jinji to provide him with a sense of stability and hope amidst the chaos of the world. The lyrics depict how Jinji's presence provides the singer with a sense of hope and happiness, and how he fears losing her.

▶

Duvet

Bôa

The song “Duvet” by Bôa is about someone describing the feeling of being overwhelmed by emotions and struggling to cope. The lyrics speak of a person struggling to understand why the relationship has ended, and how all the fears and doubts that come with it can weigh heavily.

▶

when you sleep

my bloddy valentine

“When You Sleep” by My Bloody Valentine is about feeling uncertain and confused as one gazes into a loved one’s eyes. The song expresses profound emotions which arise when the narrator can’t tell what is real, but still, the person’s beauty manages to make them laugh and smile.

▶

Fade into You

Mazzy Star

"Fade Into You" is a song about a person who is going through a difficult time, but finds solace in the presence and understanding of another person. Wishes to connect with this person, not just for comfort, but for companionship and a deeper understanding between them.
